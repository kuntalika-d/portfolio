Swapify

Shopping today is heavily influenced by trends, social media, and platform nudges, often leading young consumers into cycles of impulsive buying and regret. Through research, this project uncovered a gap between users’ intention to shop mindfully and their actual behavior, shaped by lack of clarity, trust, and decision support.

Swapify was designed as a community-driven thrifting platform that makes swapping simple, trustworthy, and socially engaging. By prioritizing clarity, safety, and ease of use—while gradually introducing features like styling, nudges, and AI support—the solution encourages more mindful, sustainable fashion choices without restricting self-expression

Mutzic

Mutzic is a vibrotactile wearable designed to make music accessible beyond sound. Developed as part of the Smart Product Design course, it translates music into structured vibration patterns that users can feel through their body.

Music today is built for listening, leaving deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals excluded from this shared emotional experience. Mutezic addresses this gap by reimagining music as a tactile experience—allowing users to sense rhythm, intensity, and mood through vibrations.

The project combines embedded systems, interaction design, and inclusive thinking to create a calming, intuitive, and accessible way to experience music.

Digi Pawket

Digi Pawket is a centralised digital platform that helps pet owners store, manage, and share all essential information about their pets in one place. Today, pet records like vaccination certificates, medical history, and insurance documents are often scattered across clinics or kept in physical formats, making them hard to access when needed—especially during emergencies or travel. This app solves that by offering a secure document vault, smart reminders for health and care routines, and controlled sharing with vets, boarding facilities, and authorities. It also includes features like QR-based identification for lost pets and travel support with regulations and document readiness. The goal is to make pet care more organised, accessible, and stress-free by ensuring that all critical information is always available when it matters most.
